Let's build together !

My passion about job growth led me to policy work at Jonhs Hopkins-School of Advanced International Studies(SAIS) in Washington DC. Job growth means a lot to me, because I believe that it is the best way to empower communities, enable individuals to help themselves and to stand with dignity. After three years, I find that real change makers are entrepreneurs, while government policies can be conducive. That was a pivot point.

I found a new channel for my passion: supporting entrepreneurial eco-system. Today, I am a ruby on rails fullstack developer after 10 week coding bootcamp at NEXT academy. I am excited that this newly found tech ability enables me to explore and build what is possible together with intrepid entrepreurs. So, let's build together!
